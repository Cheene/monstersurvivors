About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of challenging monsters. Test your skills, upgrade your abilities, and see how long you can survive in this thrilling adventure.

Game Features

Endless Adventure

Face increasingly challenging waves of monsters in an endless survival experience.

Character Development

Upgrade your abilities and unlock new powers as you progress through the game.

Strategic Combat

Plan your moves and use various tactics to overcome different types of monsters.

High Score Challenge

Compete for the highest survival time and challenge yourself to beat your records.
